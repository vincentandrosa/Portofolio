About us

History

How was M & T formed? M & T was formed because on year 2014 we saw many clothes product in the market from alot of clothes brand that gave an unfair prices according to the materials. Because of that, M & T is inspired to give the best clothes and materials with a fair price.

Vision and Mission

M & T's vision and mission is to make tops and bottoms with quality materials and very competitive prices. And in accordance with our slogan “Give a new identity”, which means we provide a very suitable price with above average quality.
